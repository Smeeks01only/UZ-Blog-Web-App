Volleyball News - UZ Wolves

Wins and Achievements

UZ Wolves Crowned Champions in National Volleyball Tournament

The UZ Wolves volleyball team emerged victorious in the national volleyball tournament held last week. With their exceptional teamwork and skill, they dominated the competition and clinched the championship title. Congratulations to the UZ Wolves on their outstanding achievement!

UZ Wolves Player Receives MVP Award

We are proud to announce that one of our very own UZ Wolves players, Tanaka Hwara, has been awarded the Most Valuable Player (MVP) in the recent regional Zimbabwe volleyball league. Tanaka's exceptional performance and contributions to the team have been recognized and celebrated. Well done, Tanaka!

UZ Wolves Announce New Head Coach

The UZ Wolves volleyball team is delighted to welcome our new head coach, Mr Gift Chirebvu. Coach Gift brings a wealth of experience and knowledge to the team and is committed to taking the UZ Wolves to new heights. We look forward to an exciting season under his leadership.
