The UZ Handball Team has emerged as the champions in the highly competitive National Handball Championship. Congratulations to the UZ Handball Team on this remarkable achievement!

We are proud to announce that Sarah Johnson, one of our talented UZ Handball players, has been honored with the Best Goalkeeper Award in the regional handball tournament.
