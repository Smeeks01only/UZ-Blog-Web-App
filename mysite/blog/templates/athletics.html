Athletics News - UZ Cheetars

Wins and Achievements

UZ Cheetars Sprinter Breaks National Record

We are thrilled to announce that John Davis, a member of the UZ Cheetars athletics team, has shattered the national record in the 100m sprint. With lightning-fast speed and determination, John clocked an impressive time of 9.87 seconds, surpassing the previous record. This remarkable achievement has put the UZ Cheetars and John in the spotlight of athletics excellence.

UZ Cheetars Long Jumper Wins Gold at International Championship

We are proud to announce that Emily Thompson, a talented long jumper from the UZ Cheetars athletics team, has secured a gold medal at the prestigious International Athletics Championship. Emily's exceptional technique and remarkable leap of 7.25 meters earned her the top spot on the podium. Congratulations to Emily and the UZ Cheetars for this outstanding achievement!

UZ Cheetars Relay Team Sets New University Record

The UZ Cheetars relay team, consisting of Sarah Johnson, Michael Thompson, Emma Davis, and James Wilson, has set a new university record in the 4x400m relay event. With seamless baton exchanges and incredible speed, they completed the race in a record-breaking time of 3 minutes and 12 seconds. This achievement showcases the team's exceptional coordination and dedication.
