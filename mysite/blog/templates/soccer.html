Uz Stars: Lighting up the Soccer Field

A Legacy of Excellence

The University of Zimbabwe's soccer team, the Uz Stars, has carved a niche for itself as one of the most dominant forces in Zimbabwean university soccer. Their unwavering passion, exceptional talent, and unwavering dedication have earned them a reputation for excellence and a trophy cabinet brimming with achievements.

A Journey Through Time

The Uz Stars' journey began in 1982, when a group of passionate soccer enthusiasts at the University of Zimbabwe came together to form a team. Despite facing initial challenges and limited resources, their determination to succeed never wavered. Their efforts paid off, and the Uz Stars quickly established themselves as a formidable force in university soccer.

A Legacy of Victories

  • 12-time National University Soccer Champions
  • Winners of the Southern African University Soccer Championship (2016, 2019, 2021)
  • Producers of multiple national soccer team players
